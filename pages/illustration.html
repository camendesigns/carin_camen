Carillu

The Adventures of Carillu


Carillu and her best friend Froggie, meet new friends while learning about honest, sharing, integrity and making friends.

Carillu is my latest project. It features Froggie, a blue frog that find out he has to move to the big city with his parents and is sad to have to leave his friends. He wonders what his life will be like and if he will be able to make new friends.

The Beginning

The Creation of Carillu and Froggie


Carillu and Froggie were created to help me dig into my childlike creativity to expand my design skills. As I began to create these characters, they came to life. I decided to use Carillu and Froggie to teach life skills to small children. Principles of honest, sharing and being nice to others are taught in a fun and creative way.

Froggie

Where it all Began


My first character was Froggie. A young blue frog that was sad when he found out his parents were moving them to the city. He didn't want to leave his friends and was worried about finding new friends.

The Evolution

Going Digital


Carillu was created when Froggie entered the Digital World. Froggie would become one of many friends of Carillu that would teach young children lifeskills.

As this project has evolved, Carillu.com was development. Future projects will include online games and mobile application.
