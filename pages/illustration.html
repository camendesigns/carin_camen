Carillu

The Adventures of Carillu


Carillu and her best friend Froggie, meet new friends while learning about honest, sharing, integrity and being kind to others.

Carillu is my latest project. It features Froggie, a blue frog that finds out he has to move to the big city with his parents and is sad to have to leave his friends. He wonders what his life will be like and if he will be able to make new friends.

The Beginning

The Creation of Carillu and Froggie


Carillu and Froggie were developed when I challenged myself to explore design through a child's eyes. As I began to create these characters and their friends, they came to life. I decided to use Carillu and Froggie to teach life skills to small children. Principles of honest, sharing, kindness and acceptance of others around us are taught in a fun and creative way.

Froggie

Where it all Began


My first character was Froggie. A young blue frog that was sad when he found out his parents were moving them to the city. He didn't want to leave his friends and was worried about finding new friends.

The Character

Developing 3 Dimensional


Once Froggie was developed in Illustrator, it was time to bring him to life. Using polymer clay a 5" version of Froggie was created. Packaging for the Froggie toy was designed and developed. Finally, 1.5" game pieces were then created in polymer clay.

The Story

Froggie's Adventure


Writing and illustrating a children's book continued the evolution of Froggie. The first draft, stimulated the creation of a series of stories around Froggie and his friends.

Print Process

Froggie and Friends


The is a lost art of interaction with those around us due to our attention peering into a screen. Developing a board game with game pieces encourages children to play interactively with others.

The Evolution

Going Digital


With the three dimensional complete, it was time to go digital.

Carillu.com brings Froggie and his friends life lessons to a broader audience. Future projects will include online games and mobile application.
