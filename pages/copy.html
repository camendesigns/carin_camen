The Next Phase of Development


The next phase of development is to build an online log in system that will contain VMRT Tracker and VMRT Planner. VMRT Tracker will be a log in system that will provide the user to input their numbers and track their activities that are in the 2 month training course. This will allow the user to track their activity and progress.



VMRT Planner will be a part of the same log in system. It will have pre-formatted plans to implement in prospecting for jobs. The user will be able to input the name and contact information of the employment contacts, maintain notes about conversations, schedule upcoming events, schedule next time to contact and put each prospect into a pre-formatted prospecting plan. This will assist the user in maintaining their pipeline of employment opportunities.



Each time the user logs in, they will be prompted with their progress, the number ratio and provided a "to do" list of activities they should accomplish that day. This list will incorporate the activities in the course and their scheduled prospecting activities.



VMRT TrakPlan will be a print version of the online program. This will also be available for those who prefer to track their activity manually rather than digitally .
