View My Resume Today


View My Resume Today is a two month training course that I wrote and developed. Its target audience is the long-term unemployed, but it is beneficial to anyone who is wanting to improve their prospecting and interview skills.

This course has a three-fold approach. Consulting Corner discusses the different personality types and how to utilize this information in the interview. Tips and Tools contains motivational and sales techniques used by professionals. The Training Center is the course platform. It contains a daily schedule combining course material, assignments, exercises and real life tasks. Here you are taught how to increase your online presence, marketing, networking and how to put them together to achieve your employment goals.

Consulting Corner In today’s competitive job market, the need to fine tune your skills have never been greater. Consulting Corner is like having your own personal consultant, guiding you on the nuances of setting yourself ahead of your competition.

Tips and Tools Let’s face it, job searching is no different than a sales position. You are in the business of selling yourself to potential employers. Learn the insider tips and tools of prospecting, interviewing, and lead followup.

Training Center The best players have coaches. Our training series provides you with coaching without the cost. Our daily and weekly activities assists you in staying motivated and focused. Activity charts help you track your progress.
