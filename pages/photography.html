Photography

Looking through the Lens


Cariluzen is the photography arm of Camen Designs. Photography is the art of looking through the lens and finding a new perspective on ordinary subject material.

My love of photography began when I placed my macro lens onto my camera and began to explore the world around me in a new perspective. The beauty of the tiniest details that normally would go unnoticed open up into this incredible visual art and beauty. Capturing the ordinary and making it appear interesting so the viewer will take a moment out of their busy day, is a challenging, but essential process. When we are bombarded with visual stimulation the competition for attention is extremely high.

Photography

A Continual Exploration


Exploring different perspectives through the lens of my camera allows me to gain greater insight to the world of design. Mother Nature is the master of design. Through her eyes, I have found the possibilities in design to be endless.

Throughout my photography and design work, one can see my journey of exploration. Capturing the art and elements of design that are found in natural and man made designs provide visual interest as one gains a greater appreciation for the world in which we reside.
