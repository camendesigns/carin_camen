Web

Design for Appeal
Develop for Interaction


Web design allows the viewer a front row seat into the designer's global gallery.
Web development creates a positive user experience drawing them into the application.

Web design and development allows me to interact with both sides of the brain, designing with the right and developing with the left. My process includes planning and development of site maps and wire frames with OmniGraffle, visual creation in Photoshop, execution utilizing WebStorm, Mamp and GitHub.

Design

Creating Visual Interest
with Common Subjects


Design is a creative visualization of all that the world has to offer.

Design allows me to challenge my creativity and push myself to diversify my designs. It is the foundation in which I build upon. I enjoy the process of assisting the client in developing what they visualize but may struggle to put into words.

Copy

Write to Express
Style to Impress


It is the harmony of copy and visual that draws us in and establishes action.

There is an expression that a picture speaks a thousands words. One word can envision a thousand pictures. A word as simple as "orange" can cause a picture of a fruit, a color, an object or a memory of person, place or event. Words motivate or de-motivate. The power of words explode when they are in combination with design.

Illustration

Enter the World
With no Limits


Illustration brings fantasy into reality and reminds us of the pleasures in being a child.

Our world is filled with illustration, taking us on a journey outside of our day in and day out routine. Releasing us into fantasy for a moment of respite from the hectic moments in life. Regardless of age, we desire to regain the moments as a child when we sat back and just day dreamed of the possibilities beyond our imagination.

Photography

Exploring Beyond
The Human Eye


Photography is the ability to see the world through a different perspective.

Looking through the lens of a camera I was opened up a whole new world for me to explore. Through a macro lens, I find beauty in the strangest places. Have you seen how amazing a mud puddle is? Once I viewed the reflection from a raindrop falling off a delicate flower's petal, I began to see the world in a new perspective.
